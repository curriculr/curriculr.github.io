--- layout: front title: Open platform for interactive learning ---
A single-entry platform for both instructors and students.
Interactive learning: in-video quizzing, assessments, surveys, and graded discussions.
Support for public and private (by invitation only) classes.
Out of the box English and Arabic locales; others can easily be added.
Up to date progress reports for both students and instructors.
Access to visualized collected data on class and site activities.

and much more


  • Courses are organized into units and lectures with videos, audios, images, and documents. Students can enroll in classes, participate in discussions, attend lectures, and attempt quizzes and exams.
  • A multi-tenant platform; tenants are identified by sub-domains. Each tenant acts as if it is a separate site with its own configuration, courses, classes, users and theme.
  • Themeable interface; it supports themes and theme inheritance. Adding a new theme can be as easy as providing a name and changing a few CSS style classes.
  • Extensible platform; it was designed to be extensible by means of other gems, plugins or mountable engines; just like any other Rails application.
